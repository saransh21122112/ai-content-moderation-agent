The problem

Content moderation is
breaking your platform

Every growing social platform hits the same wall. Content flies faster than your team can review it — and the consequences are real.

Harmful content stays up too long

A post that goes viral in 10 minutes can sit unreviewed for hours. By the time your team acts, the damage is done — to your users and your reputation.

Human moderators are burning out

Reviewing hundreds of pieces of disturbing content daily takes a severe mental toll. Turnover is high, consistency drops, and costs keep rising.

Good content gets wrongly removed

Blunt keyword filters and overworked reviewers make mistakes — blocking legitimate creators, triggering appeals, and eroding user trust.

Advertisers pull spend

Brand safety issues caused by unmoderated content lead to advertiser boycotts — the kind that cost platforms hundreds of millions and rarely fully recover.

How Sentinel works

Three layers. One decision.
Under 2 seconds.

Sentinel doesn't just flag content — it thinks through it, explains it, and only escalates to humans when it genuinely needs to.

01

Instant triage

The moment content is submitted, a fast AI agent reads it and classifies the risk level — safe, suspicious, or critical — in under 200ms.

~200ms
02

Deep analysis

For anything that isn't clearly safe, a second AI agent reads the full context — intent, tone, who it targets — and produces a final decision with a plain-English explanation.

~1.5s total
03

Human review — only when needed

Decisions below 85% confidence are automatically held for human review. Your team sees only the genuinely hard cases — not thousands of obvious ones.

Edge cases only

What's included

Everything your Trust & Safety
team actually needs

Not just an API. A complete moderation infrastructure your team can operate from day one.

Real-time moderation

Text, images, and uploaded video files reviewed and decided in under 2 seconds — fast enough to act before content spreads.

Video & image scanning

AWS Rekognition scans every image and video for violence, weapons, and explicit content — before the AI pipeline even runs.

Human review queue

Low-confidence decisions land in a dedicated queue. Reviewers see the AI's reasoning and can accept or override with a single click.

User appeals

Users can appeal any decision. The AI re-reads the content alongside the appeal reason and produces a fresh recommendation for your team.

Analytics & trends

Daily volume charts, action distribution, category breakdown, and false positive rates — everything you need to tune and report on performance.

Full audit trail

Every decision, every human override, every appeal — logged with timestamps, AI reasoning, and reviewer notes. Compliance-ready out of the box.
